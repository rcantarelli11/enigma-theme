## The Story

### We started Enigma because we were tired of the same thing.

Every supplement brand screams the loudest, slaps a celebrity on the label, and hopes you don't flip the bottle around. Proprietary blends. Underdosed ingredients. $60 tubs of marketing.

We wanted something different. Something honest.

Enigma was built on a simple idea: if the product is good, you don't need to hide what's in it. Every formula is fully transparent — every ingredient, every dose, right on the label. No fillers. No blends. No games.

### Premium doesn't mean overpriced.

We believe premium means better ingredients, better formulas, and better results — not a bigger price tag for the same powder in a flashier package. Enigma is priced to be the best value in its class, not the cheapest on the shelf.

### Built for the people who show up.

Not influencers. Not sponsored athletes. Regular people who train consistently, eat with intention, and want supplements that match their discipline. That's who Enigma is for.
